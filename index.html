My Mission

I started this job with the goal of being able to help every parent, new or repeat, have the safest and most comfortable birth possible. I want to make sure that you start your new journey on the right foot, knowing that your new baby is safe and happy.

What I can do!

Wether in the hospital or in your home, I'm here to help you and your baby stay healthy mentally and physically in this new step in your lives together. Thats why I offer both Labor and Postpartum doula services. I'll be there to support and advocate for you through all the stress and confusion of pregnancy.

BIRTH DOULA
I can be there, right by your side, making sure you get the best birth experience possible. Together we'll discuss what your boundaries and prefrences are, wether you want to be in a hospital or a birth center, if you want a standing birth or in bed, and we can talk about all your best options. And when the time comes I can be there to make sure that no matter how stressful it may get that you dont get pushed into any uncomfortable situations.

POSTPARTUM CARE
You did it! Your baby is here in the world, and you're ready to go home and start your life together. Wether your a new parent or if you've done this ten times already, transitioning your baby to their new home can be stressful. If you need help with the sleepless nights, or if you just need a minute to yourself to get back to 100%, you can call on me to be there to support you through it. I offer both overnight and daytime services to help make sure that you and baby get everything you need, even if that's just peace of mind.

Who am I?

I'm a mother with a degree in early childhood development. I love working with children and am passionate about making sure that parents are taken care of as well as their children.
